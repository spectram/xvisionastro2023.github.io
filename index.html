WELCOME TO XVISION-2023!

This Capacity Building Workshop aims to provide an unique platforms for the students and yound researchers from developing nations to learn the handling of X-ray data from the missions like XMM-Newton, NICER and NuSTAR. The teams of experts include a number of renowned senior lectures from various fields. The hands-on sessions are supposed to be lead by the respective instrument team members. This workshop is scheduled for Februry 06-17, 2023 and will be held at North-West University, Potchefstroom Campus, Potchefstroom, South Africa.

This workshop is the first kind where International Astronomical Union ( IAU) and Committee on Space Research (COSPAR) are jointly supporting such an intiative for the capacity building in Astronomy. This workshop is also supported generously by the South African Gamma-ray Astronomy Programme (SA-GAMMA) and Center for Space Research, North West University, Potchefstroom , NASA and European Space Agency (ESA) in various forms.

The Lectures and hands-on sessions shall revolves around the following major scientific areas, but are by no means limited to:

  • The ionized outflows from black-hole objects
  • The spin, mass and nature of accretion disk in Stellar mass Black holes and AGNs
  • The X-ray emission from Blazars and connection with the TeV emission from jets
  • Thermal X-ray emission from Galaxy Clusters
  • X-ray emission from Pulsars
  • Multi-Messenger Transients
  • Multimedia content distribution
